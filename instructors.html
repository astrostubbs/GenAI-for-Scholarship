Site Analytics (GoatCounter)

All pages on this site are tracked using GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly, cookie-free analytics service. It tracks page views and outbound link clicks (including "Add to Calendar" buttons and notebook downloads).

Open Analytics Dashboard

What you can see in the dashboard:

  • Page views: Which pages are visited most, with daily/weekly/monthly trends
  • Outbound clicks: Clicks on external links, including "Add to Calendar" buttons and Google Colab links
  • Referrers: Where visitors are coming from (direct links, email, etc.)
  • Browsers and devices: What platforms students are using
Note: GoatCounter does not use cookies and does not track individual users. All data is aggregate. The dashboard is publicly readable at the URL above — no login required. To change settings, log in at genaiatfas.goatcounter.com/settings.
